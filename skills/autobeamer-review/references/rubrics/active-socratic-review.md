# Active-Socratic Review Rubric

Review `active-socratic` decks as mentor-led discovery paths.

## Critical Checks

- Each major idea is motivated by a question, challenge, or thought experiment.
- The learner is asked to attempt the key step before the deck reveals the answer.
- Hints are staged so the first hint preserves agency and the final hint makes progress possible.
- The mentor voice asks precise questions rather than giving vague encouragement.
- Reflection prompts help the learner notice what they discovered.

## Failure Modes

- Giving exposition before the learner has a reason to want it.
- Asking questions that are either impossible without missing background or too trivial to create insight.
- Omitting delayed solutions, leaving the learner stuck without closure.
- Turning Socratic mode into a quiz instead of a guided reconstruction.
